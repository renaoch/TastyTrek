Our Story

WHERE EVERY
BITE TELLS
A STORY

Tasty Trek was born with a simple dream — to bring bold, authentic flavours to the streets of Guwahati. Located at GandhiBasti Tiniali, we serve everything from street-style Momos to hearty Main Course dishes, every single day of the year.

Our kitchen is built on honest ingredients, generous portions, and the warmth you'd feel at home. Whether it's a quick morning paratha or a late-night momo run — we're always here.
